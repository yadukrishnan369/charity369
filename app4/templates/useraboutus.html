Donate For Better World


Charity web app aims to reduce waste through donations . People can donate anything such as food , clothing , house appliances , furniture , study materials etc . to those who are in need . they can register to the system by providing basic details . The charity organizations collect the donations from the donators and make it available to the needed ones . In party functions there may be possibilities for food wastage . Here the donor or public can inform welfare and charity organizations through this system for collecting the leftover foods . And the welfare and charity organizations leftover the foods may reach those who are in need . This web application is also aimed to provide cash donations to charity organizations .

A charity is an organization which raises money in order to help people who are sick or very poor, or who have a disability. ... If you give money to charity, you give it to one or more charitable organizations. If you do something for charity, you do it in order to raise money for one or more charitable organizations.

Charity is the act of giving help to those in need of it. It is a humanitarian act. It involves giving money, goods or time and effort to those who need it. It is done without expecting something in return. Giving money or food to poor people is an example of charity.
